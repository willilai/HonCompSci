- learn more about them. If you learn more, you will know more key aspects about their survival. Once you know more, you know what is the best way to help save them.
- limit your carbon emissions. higher temperature makes their food go toward the poles, making the whales traveling farther, limiting the amount of food they will eat. The less food they eat, the harder it is to survive the winter months.
